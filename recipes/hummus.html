Extra Easy Hummus

Hummus is a Middle-Eastern spread/dip made from chickpeas.

It's a great side dish for almost anything. Hummus goes well with most appetizers and is frequently used as a dipping sauce with bread.

Ingredients

4 servings

  • 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained, liquid reserved
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor combine garbanzo beans, garlic, cumin, salt and olive oil. Blend on low speed, gradually adding reserved bean liquid, until desired consistency is achieved.
  2. Serve and enjoy!
